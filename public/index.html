Eye Training Exercises | Visual Acuity Improvement | BlinkCamp

BlinkCamp is a free and open-source website for eye training exercises, with customizable routines that can help improve visual acuity and reaction times. Our website features a dot that moves around with many different routines that you can choose from, as well as options to make it faster or smaller.

Whether you're looking to improve your reaction time, reduce eye strain, or just want a fun way to exercise your eyes, BlinkCamp has something for you. Our website is great for people of all ages and skill levels.
